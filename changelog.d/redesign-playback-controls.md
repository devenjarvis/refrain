### Changed

- Agent status symbols now use playback-control glyphs: `▷` (starting), `▶` (active), `⏺` (waiting/on-mic), `⏸` (idle), `⏭` (done), `⏹` (error), reinforcing Refrain's music identity.
- Session rows display a `⏏` suffix while Haiku is composing a branch name, disappearing automatically once the rename completes or fails.
